Data Deletion Instructions

Updated: January 2026

Annos does not collect, store, or process any personal data on its servers. All your data — including food logs, calorie and nutrition data, and user preferences — is stored exclusively on your device.

How to delete your data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all your Annos data by following these steps:

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to General → iPhone Storage
  3. Find and select Annos
  4. Tap Delete App

This will permanently remove all data associated with Annos from your device. Since Annos does not store any data on external servers, no further action is required.

Advertising and analytics data

Annos uses Mixpanel for analytics and Tenjin for ad attribution. These services may collect anonymous device identifiers. You can opt out of ad tracking at any time by going to Settings → Privacy → Tracking on your iOS device.

To request deletion of any anonymous analytics data, please contact us at annos@mail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at annos@mail.com.
